Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

PetLog is developed by the Abraham Oladotun Foundation NFP ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use PetLog.

Data We Collect

PetLog is designed with privacy first. All your data stays on your device.

  • Pet profiles, health records, vaccination schedules, medication logs, vet visits, and weight history are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.
  • We do not require account creation or login.

Sensitive Data

PetLog stores pet health information you provide, including vaccination records, medication schedules, and veterinary visit history. This data is kept exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You may delete this data at any time from Settings.

Third-Party Services

PetLog uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Apple App Store or Google Play. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The free version of PetLog displays ads via Google AdMob. You can remove ads by subscribing to PetLog Premium. We request non-personalized ads by default. On iOS, you may be asked for tracking permission under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

In-App Purchases

PetLog offers optional premium subscriptions processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly handle any payment information. All billing is managed by Apple or Google per their respective terms.

Children's Privacy

PetLog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time from Settings > Delete All Data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@abrahamoladotun.org
